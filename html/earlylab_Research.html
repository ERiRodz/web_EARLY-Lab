    Research

    Our Focus

    Our research focuses on advancing early childhood by attending to accessibility and developmental appropriateness of assessments that reflect the needs of diverse learners, improving psychometric methods to support test score use and interpretation, and applying effective assessment practices to better support students from diverse backgrounds. Core tenets include:

    • Evidence-Centered Design
    • Constructing Measures
    • Ecological Models
